Average U.S Citizen Finances

In 2024, the average salary of an American citizen in the U.S. is $63,795, according to the latest data from the Social Security Administration. How your salary compares to others will depend on your industry and skilI set, as you'd expect.

More Information

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the Consumer Price Index (CPI) for 2024 shows a 2.7% increase compared to 2023, indicating a rise in consumer prices over the year;

  • Food prices: Food prices were 2.4% higher than in November 2023
  • Shelter prices: The shelter index increased 0.3% in November, accounting for nearly 40% of the monthly increase.
  • Prices for all items excluding food and energy increased by 3.3% over the 12-month period.

In conclusion, the price of food & other expenses are increasing at a rate faster than the salaries of the average citizen in the U.S.
